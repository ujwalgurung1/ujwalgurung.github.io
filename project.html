Home: Shelter or Asset?


Rising house prices are also causing a housing affordability crisis in other countries, but at the same time they are increasing homeowners’ wealth. Governments are faced with the challenge to encourage households to build up housing wealth while also promoting access to affordable housing. Housing is the principal source of household wealth in most countries around the world. Households in the top of the wealth distribution hold more diversified portfolios, less wealthy households own mostly housing and least wealthy households own virtually nothing with some even is net debt.

Homeownership and Social Moblity


Promoting homeownership has long been a policy objective. This has often been justified on grounds that homeownership can help households accumulate wealth and save for retirement. However, governemnt policies tend to have unwanted effects such as impacting social mobility. Social mobility refers to the movement of individuals either up or down the socioeconomic ladder, such as a low-income family moving up to become a part of the middle class. The chart shows that countries with high levels of of social mobility exhibit lower levels of home ownership.

Conclusion


To conclude home ownership and Social Mobility are weakly negatively correlated. Even if wealth inequality is lower where homeownership is more widespread, encouraging homeownership is not the appropriate policy response to help low and middle-income families accumulate wealth. Promoting residential mobility is an important policy challenge, especially in England where there is a large price gap between regions. This means that there could be labour market–skills mismatches where people from lower house price regions cannot move to a higher one. Mobility can cushion labour-market shocks and reduce regional inequalities – for instance by alleviating local imbalances between labour demand and supply and by promoting the optimal match between workers and jobs. This project stimulates further in the fact the School score aspect of the overall score showed a higher albeit still weak link. This relationship should be explored further.
